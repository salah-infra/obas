## 2. National Hospitality Security Integration Platform
**Smart integration between hotels, furnished apartments & security authorities
(Saudi Arabia, since 1994)**

Since 1994, an OBAS founder has led the design, implementation, and operation of
large-scale hospitality security integration projects in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia —
a unified ecosystem digitally connecting hotels and furnished apartments with
governmental security entities.

Built around a **highly scalable middleware and integration platform** that connects with
existing Hotel Management Systems (HMS/PMS), eliminating duplicate data entry while
preserving operational efficiency.

**Security authority platform features:**
- Centralized blacklist management with controlled add/remove and full audit trails
- Intelligent search & investigation tools
- Real-time alerts & notifications
- Live guest monitoring across connected facilities
- Historical guest movement tracking
- Centralized dashboards, reports & analytics

**Smart matching & risk detection:** configurable matching rules, multi-level
verification logic, percentage-based similarity detection, partial & fuzzy matching, and
risk classification — identifying potential matches instantly on guest registration or
departure.

**Expandable national ecosystem:** architected for future integration with tourist car
rental offices, gold & jewelry shops, tourism & leisure resorts, and other regulated
activities.

**Strategic value delivered:** national-scale digital transformation, enhanced public
safety and operational visibility, reduced manual procedures and errors, improved
compliance, and a scalable architecture supporting future smart-city initiatives.

## 3. MAQAM — National Umrah Hospitality & Transportation Platform
**A government digital booking ecosystem for Umrah services**

An OBAS founder contributed to the creation and implementation of **MAQAM**, a pioneering
national digital ecosystem in Saudi Arabia — one of the first large-scale national Umrah
reservation platforms in the Arab region and developing world — enabling pilgrims
worldwide to book hotels, transportation, and tourism services through a unified,
government-regulated ecosystem.

**Strategic vision:** digitize the entire Umrah hospitality journey, enhance transparency
and trust in e-bookings, protect pilgrims from fraud, centralize tourism operational
visibility, retain tourism revenue within the Saudi economy, and enable smarter tourism
planning.

**Hospitality CRS ecosystem:** room inventory, dynamic/seasonal/group pricing, electronic
contracting, hotel policy & media management, reservation management, real-time
availability synchronization, barcode/QR, and multi-branch/multi-property operations.

**Public reservation experience:** search by classification/location/dates, rich visual
hotel content, price & facility comparison, intelligent filtering, secure online
payments, instant confirmations, and dedicated mobile apps for pilgrims globally.

**Integrated mobility & group travel:** transportation reservation platform for tourist
vehicles, intercity transport, chauffeur services, and bus fleets — supporting
individuals, families, tourism companies, and organized Umrah groups, with bulk bus
booking and dynamic capacity management.

**National tourism intelligence:** nationwide hotel availability, transportation capacity
visibility, occupancy rates, reservation analytics, seasonal demand forecasting, and
operational dashboards — creating a **live digital tourism map for Saudi Arabia.**

**Visa integration & national impact:** the Umrah visa process was linked directly to
confirmed hotel reservations, verified transportation bookings, and approved tourism
services — ensuring authenticity, pilgrim protection, and reduced fraud, while keeping
tourism revenue inside Saudi Arabia and reducing dependency on external intermediaries.

**Ecosystem integrations:** banking systems, card processors, government services,
